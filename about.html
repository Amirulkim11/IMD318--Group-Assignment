About Us

Our story, heritage, and purpose

Who We Are

OldTown White Coffee is a Malaysian brand founded to preserve the authentic taste of traditional kopitiam coffee. We bring the original white coffee experience to modern consumers while respecting its heritage.

The name “OldTown” represents nostalgia, tradition, and the warmth of Malaysian coffee culture.

Business Overview

OldTown White Coffee operates outlets across Malaysia and overseas, offering Malaysian food and beverages. Our business focuses on quality, consistency, and customer satisfaction.
